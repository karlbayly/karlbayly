Emotional Intelligence

What is Emotional Intelligence?

Emotional Intelligence the perception, use, understand, of our personal emotions.

How is it different to IQ?

I would argue that is a quite different from IQ as a persons IQ a.k. human intelligence is a score derived from a set of standardised tests and not everyone test well, but that dose not mean they have a low or high IQ. If you were well equipped with a solid EQ I believe you are better equipped to go thought life than someone with a super high IQ.

Why is it important?

A persons EQ effects every aspect of life. Each daily interaction with others is reflected in their EQ , how we react, reflect, engage with other life forms and how we respond to our own needs and others with empathy and kindness. I mean the if everyone interacted well with these 5 elements (Self-awareness, Self-regulation, Social skill , Empathy, Motivation) the world would probably be in better shape.
